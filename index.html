About The Company

Building Mato Grosso do Sul

Located in Campo Grande, Mato Grosso do Sul, Concreto & Design Ltda is a dedicated construction firm specializing in the nonresidential sector. Our name reflects our core philosophy: combining the durability of "Concreto" (Concrete) with the precision of technical "Design".

Under the leadership of Ana Paula Da Silva Araripe, we bring a disciplined approach to industrial and commercial building. We understand that functional spaces must be resilient, compliant, and optimized for productivity.

Our mission is to support the growth of Campo Grande by providing high-quality infrastructure that meets the rigorous demands of the modern industrial market.
